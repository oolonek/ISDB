ISDB

A database of In-Silico predicted MS/MS spectrum of Natural Products

Welcome to ISDB GitHub Page.

Here you will find the full In-Silico Database (ISDB) described in the paper "Integration of Molecular Networking and In-Silico MS/MS Fragmentation for Natural Products Dereplication". This ISDB contains predicted MS/MS fragmentation spectrum in positive ion mode for 170 602 Natural Products (NPs) found in the UNPD (Universal Natural Products Database).
